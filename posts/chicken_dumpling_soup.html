Chicken and Dumpling Soup

The recipe is adapted from Bohemian Vegan Kitchen.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup of dried soy curls
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 7 cups of broth
For the dumplings:
  • 1 cup (120 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (120 mL) plant milk
  • 2 Tbsp (28 grams) of oil
Instructions
  1. Soak the soy curls in boiling water for 5 minutes. Drain the soy curls, squeeze out the excess water, and chop into small pieces. Set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat up the olive oil. Saute the onion, carrots, and celery.
  3. Add the spices, broth, and soy curls. Bring to a boil.
  4. Make the dumpling dough - donâ€™t overmix.
  5. When the soup is boiling, plop in spoonfuls of dumpling dough. Cook for 10-15 minutes until the dumplings are firm.
Notes
  • Feel free to use chickpeas instead of soy curls.
