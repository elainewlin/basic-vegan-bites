Banana Oat Pancakes

The recipe is a good way to use up one overripe banana.

Makes 9 pancakes

Ingredients
  • 1 overripe banana
  • 1/2 cup of plant milk
  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1/2 Tbsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • pinch of salt
  • plant butter for cooking
Instructions
  1. Put all of the ingredients in a blender. Blend to combine.
  2. Heat 1 tsp of plant butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. When the pan is hot, plop in 4 pancakes.
  4. Cover with a lid, and cook for 3 minutes. When the tops are set, flip the pancakes over. Cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  5. Repeat until youâ€™ve cooked all of the batter.
Notes
  • The pancakes will be thin.
