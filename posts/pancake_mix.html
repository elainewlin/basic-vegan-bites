Pancake Mix

Makes ~7 cups of mix (~56 pancakes)

For the mix:
  • 480 grams all-purpose flour
  • 240 grams whole wheat flour
  • 54 grams sugar
  • 50 grams ground flaxseed
  • 40 grams baking powder
  • 5 grams of sea salt
For pancakes (makes 12):
  • 180 grams of mix
  • 270 grams of plant milk
  • 1/2 Tbsp of vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. Make the pancake batter. Don't overmix.
  2. Heat 1 tsp of plant butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. When the pan is hot, plop in 4 pancakes.
  4. Cook the pancakes, ~3 minutes per side.
