Coffee Protein Smoothie

This recipe is inspired by Epic Mint Leaves. It sounds suspicious, but tastes quite nice.

Makes 1 smoothie

Ingredients
  • 80 g black beans
  • 25 g peanut butter
  • 2 dates
  • 120 mL plant milk
  • 120 mL coffee
  • 1 tsp of flax seed (optional)
Instructions
  1. Blend together all of the ingredients.
  2. Pour over ice, and serve immediately.
